About Me

I am Noah Kirchner, a Washington, DC-based government consultant interested in GEOINT, Homeland Security, National & Wisconsin Politics, and Wisconsin Sports. In my current role as a Strategy & Transactions Consultant at Deloitte, I specialize in leveraging policy, data, and geospatial analysis to drive decision-making for federal government clients at the Department of Homeland Security and Department of Defense. I am a through-and-through Wisconsin native, having grown up in Milwaukee County before attending the University of Wisconsin-Madison and working for the State of Wisconsin for three years while in Madison. My portfolio consists mostly of projects created during my time as a part-time GIS master's student at UW-Madison, which I have been enrolled in since 2024.
